Rule the Realm

Rule the Realm is a turn based strategy game in which players raise earn gold to raise armies and conquer the map! Players begin with 2 gold and earn 1 gold per turn plus an additional gold for every 5 tiles they own. They can use this gold to purchase units. As units explore the map they encounter enemies. When enemies are encountered the game goes into battle mode where units are deployed onto a new hex grid against enemy units. Units that survive the battle and participated in killing enemies are able to level up and increase either their attack or defense. However, even high level units are vulnerable because attack and defense scores represent a range between 0 and that score which means that a new unit always has a chance to kill a high powered unit. Units will also encounter other races. If a player captures the base of another race they can use it to recruit troops of that race. Rule the Realm is a game of luck and strategy! Will you be able to raise an army and conquer the realm?
